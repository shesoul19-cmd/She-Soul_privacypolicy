Privacy Policy

She & Soul – Your Wellness, Your Privacy

Welcome to She & Soul. We value the trust you place in us and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

To provide a personalized wellness experience, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name and email address.
  • User Content: Journal entries or profile data you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you interact with our app features.
  • Device Data: OS version, device model, and unique identifiers.

2. Use of Information

We use the data collected to:

  • Maintain your personal account and preferences.
  • Deliver personalized content and mental health resources.
  • Improve app performance and fix technical bugs.
  • Send critical updates regarding your account or policy changes.

3. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

4. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Access and edit your profile at any time.
  • Users may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information. Requests can be submitted by emailing support@sheandsoul.co.in from the registered email address. We will process requests in accordance with applicable laws and operational requirements.
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.

5. Account Deletion Process

If you wish to delete your account and remove all personal information from our servers, please contact us:

Email: support@sheandsoul.co.in

Subject: Account Deletion Request

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.
